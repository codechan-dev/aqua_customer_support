WaterCan Delivery System - Service Overview
-------------------------------------------
WaterCan Delivery is a subscription-based 20-liter drinking water can delivery service
for homes, offices, and small businesses. The service operates from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM
every day, including weekends and public holidays.

Customers can place orders using the mobile app, website, or phone call to the call center.
Standard delivery time is 60–90 minutes within city limits. Express delivery (30–45 minutes)
is available in selected areas for an additional fee.


Product Types
-------------
1. 20L Refillable Water Can
   - Food-grade reusable plastic jar
   - Requires customers to return empty cans at the next delivery
   - Security deposit is charged for each new can issued

2. 20L Disposable Water Can
   - Single-use sealed can
   - No return required, no deposit
   - Slightly higher per-can price than refillable


Pricing & Charges
-----------------
- Base price per 20L refillable can: 80–120 INR (city-dependent)
- Base price per 20L disposable can: 110–150 INR
- Express delivery charge: 20–40 INR per order
- Night-time surcharge (after 9:00 PM): 10–20 INR per order
- Empty can security deposit (refillable only): 200–300 INR per can, refundable when can is returned

Bulk discounts may be available for offices ordering 10 or more cans per day under a monthly contract.


Order Placement Rules
---------------------
- Minimum order quantity: 1 can
- Maximum order quantity per single order: 20 cans
- Customers must share a valid delivery address and mobile number
- OTP verification may be required for first-time customers

Orders can be scheduled in advance. Customers can choose a preferred delivery slot of 1 hour
between 6:00 AM and 9:00 PM.


Subscription Plans
------------------
Customers can subscribe to weekly or monthly plans to get regular deliveries:

1. Weekly Plan
   - Fixed number of cans per week (e.g., 3, 5, or 7 cans)
   - Deliveries are auto-scheduled on selected weekdays
   - Slightly lower per-can rate than one-time orders

2. Monthly Plan
   - Fixed number of cans per month (e.g., 12, 20, or 30 cans)
   - Flexible scheduling with the option to pause for up to 10 days
   - Best discount per can compared to other options

Subscription customers get priority support and faster resolution of issues.


Delivery & Logistics
--------------------
- Delivery partners use bikes or mini-vans to deliver cans
- Each delivery partner has a maximum capacity per trip based on vehicle type
- The system assigns orders to the nearest available delivery partner
- If there is a delay beyond the promised time window, customers may receive a coupon or discount

Customers can track their order status in real time through the app:
  - Placed
  - Confirmed
  - Out for Delivery
  - Delivered
  - Cancelled


Cancellation & Refund Policy
----------------------------
- Orders can be cancelled for free up to 15 minutes after placing
- If the order is already out for delivery, a small cancellation fee may be charged
- If the delivery partner reaches the location but the customer is unavailable,
  the order may be marked as "Attempted" and a re-delivery fee may apply

Refunds for cancelled orders are processed back to the original payment method
within 3–5 working days.


Payment Methods
---------------
- Cash on Delivery (COD)
- UPI apps (e.g., PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm)
- Credit and debit cards
- Wallet balance (prepaid top-ups in the app)

For subscription customers, auto-debit can be enabled using cards or UPI mandates.


Common FAQs
-----------
Q: What is the minimum order quantity?
A: The minimum order quantity is 1 water can.

Q: Do I have to return the empty can?
A: Yes, for refillable cans you must return the empty jars. Disposable cans do not need to be returned.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery takes 60–90 minutes. Express delivery takes 30–45 minutes in supported areas.

Q: Can I pause my subscription?
A: Yes, monthly subscribers can pause their plan for up to 10 days per month.

Q: Is the water safe to drink?
A: Yes, all water is purified using RO + UV + mineralization and tested regularly as per local standards.
